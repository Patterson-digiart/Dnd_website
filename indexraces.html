Races

Who Will You Be?

In DND there is a whole host of interesting and unique playable races. Some like halflings, gnomes, dwarves, and goblins scurry about beneath the other larger races. Half orcs and goliaths are the perfect tanks, strong and foreboding. Some hail from the other planes of existence like gensai, tieflings, aasimar, and dragonborn. Or you could be boring and play a human I guess idk. Regardless of what race you pick we have an intuitive guide for how to play your new adventurer!

Backstory

Your Race will determine how others see you, what your background will be, and root you in the world of your Dungeon Master's Creation as your characters culture influences how they react to and see the world.

Humans

Its a person, its just a dude. You are (presumably) a human so if you want to play yourself or anyone else of your species this is the race for you. Humans are versatile and tend to exist everywhere, they have a +1 bonus to all abilities to show their versatile nature.

>

Elves

Elves are the graceful and magical decendants of the fae. They have sharply pointed ears, unnatural beauty and grace, and tend to be secluded to live their preternaturaly long lives in their woodland homes. Elves have +2 bonus to dexterity, darkvision, and have advantage on charm and sleep effects.


Dwarves

Dwarves are short and stout mountain folk who spend their day toiling beneath the earth unearthing its riches and using the metal they mine to create exquisite works of art. Dwarves live long lives (up to 400years) and gain a +2 to constitution and resistance to poison from their hardy physique.


Gnomes

Gnomes are tiny, excitable tinkerers who love to create things and explore the world around them. They live in secret areas, usually underground, but unlike dwarves like to come out of their holes to see the beauty of the world around them. Gnomes live long lives (up to 500 years) and have a +2 to intelligence.


Halflings

HALFLINGS ARE NOT HOBBITS. HALFLINGS ARE LEGALLY DISTINCT FROM HOBBITS. Halflings are short, have large furry feet, love to stay at home in hill houses in the shi- Generic Halfling Village and are very polite. Halflings live ~150 years and gain a +2 to dexterity. They are lucky (reroll 1s) and brave (resistant to fear.)


Tiefling

Tieflings are the children of humanoids and fiends (demons and devils) They can come in any color, grow horns out of their heads, and have forked tails. Tieflings are often feared and mistreated whereve they go and so they develop thick skin and abrassive personalities to compensate. Tieflings gain a +1 to intelligence and +2 to charisma, can cast thaumaturgy and are resistant to fire.


Aasimar

Aasimar are the children of humanoids and divine beings (gods, angels, planetar, etc.) that are placed in the world to defend goodness. They receive visions from a young age from a patron via dreams that guide them toward their destiny. Aasimar can live up to 160 years or as long as their host race, gain a +2 to charisma, and are resistant to necrotic damage.


Half Orc

Half Orcs are the children of Orcs and (usually) humans and gain the benefits from this union in their great strength and determination. They are often caught in the middle of their two ancestries too orcish for humanoids, to human for orcs (that mixed kid narrative is strong). Half Orcs gain a +1 to Constitution and +2 to Strength and rarely live longer than 75 years.


Genasi

Genasi are the children of elementals or djinn from the 4 elemental planes and as such can command water, earth, fire, or air depneding on their ancestry. Genasi are rare and almost never have contact with their elemental parents and are seen as strange to humanoid society. Genasi gain a +2 to constitution and usually live up to 120 years. They gain additional skills with their specific type.


Goliath

Goliaths are raiders decendant from giants who live in the chilly mountains. They are nomadic and periodically come down from their cool homes to raid humanoid civilizations. Goliaths believ in survival of the fittest and are very competitive in their tribes. Goliaths have a similar lifespan to humans and gain a +2 to strength and +1 to constitution.


Firbolg

Firbolgs are caretakers of the natural world and often live in secluded natural villages and tribes. They have cow-like faces and earth like fur-tones. Firbolgs gain +2 Wisdom and +1 to Strength and an live up to 500 years. Firbolgs can cast detect magic and disguise self once a rest or cast invisibility once a day.


Dragonborn

Dragonborn are dragon people. They come in the colors that dragons do (chromatic and metallic) and can use the breath weapons of the dragons of their respective color. Dragonborn lore is as varied as opinions on whether they have tails or not so go off as far as backstory is concerned. Dragonborn gain a +2 to Strength and +1 to Charisma and have a breath weapon and resistance to their damage type.


Goblin

If you live in the English Speaking world you know what a goblin is, if not Google goblin, you clearly have internet service. They are green, angry, stabby, and selfish. Goblins live in clans and are usually weak but work together. Goblins that have magic are called Booyhags because magic is rare in their societies. Goblins gain a +2 to dexterity and +1 to Constitution and live up to 60 years.

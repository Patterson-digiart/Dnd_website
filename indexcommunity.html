Community
Critical Role Dungeons GIF from Criticalrole GIFs

The DND Community

is large and far reaching. From famous writers, directors, and actors to everyday hobbyists, cosplayers, and fans. In the past few years the community has been rapidly expanding and experiencing a surge in both usership and popularity. While this growth has led to a diverse and close knit community, it can be hard for newcomers to know the whos and whats of the broader fandom. But be not discouraged! Here is a guide to the community, businesses, and a place to message all about DND 5e.

Recommended Starting Place

I recommend starting by watching Actual Play shows as a way to get the feel of the game without needing to pour through a bunch of books. Another good option is tutorial Youtube videos if a full campaign is too long for you. The PHB, while useful, is best used for small problems.

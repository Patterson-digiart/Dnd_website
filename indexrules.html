So How Does All This Work?

D&D 5th Edition is the most recent and easy to use version of the dungeons and dragon RPG system released by Wizards of the Coast. Below are links to the basic rules, The Players Handbook, Supplemental materials that have different subclasses, races, and class features, and online resources that help teach beginning players and GMs the basics of the game.

Rule #1: There Are No Rules

D&D is a game about customization and having fun, not getting bogged down in the rules. If theres a rule you dont like? Change it. Is there a system thats confusing you? Ignore it. Rules As Written are there to give your game structure and guide you on your adventure but they are not laws. You are the captain, this is your ship, and if you want to ram that ship into a dragon thats up to you.

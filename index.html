Welcome!

Behold!

Do you like fantasy? Adventure? Did you read Lord of The Rings and wish you could step inside its pages? Do you want to sit down with a group of friends and create an immersive, collaborative, and unique story together in the comfort of your own home?

Dungeons and Dragons is the Game for You!

D&D is a Tabletop Role Playing Game (TTRPG) where you and a group of friends create Player Characters (PCs) based on a set of race and class guidelines found in the Official Dungeons and Dragons Player Handbook (PHB). A separate player, known as the Dungeon Master creates a story world where the PCs live, fight, and adventure in. Over time your party will go on adventures of your own, or others, creation and have endless hours of fun and entertainment!
